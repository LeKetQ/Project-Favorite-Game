The Medallion

A witcher medallion is a silver symbol of the witchers' profession. Each one is shaped to represent the school a witcher comes from. It is sensitive to magic, vibrating and tugging on its chain when spells are being cast or magical beings, like genies or even mages, are present. This magic medallion is given to every young witcher candidate who has passed the Trial of the Grasses.
